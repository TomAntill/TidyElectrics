About Tidy Electrics

Welcome to Tidy Electrics where exceptional craftsmanship, unwavering professionalism, and a commitment to safety come together to provide you with top-notch electrical solutions. With over 5 years of experience in the electrical industry, Tidy Electrics has been serving Torquay and its surrounding communities with passion and expertise.

Why Choose Tidy Electrics?


Expertise: I am a licensed and certified electrician with extensive knowledge of the latest electrical codes and practices. You can trust that your project will be completed to the highest standards.
Safety First: Your safety is our priority. We follow industry best practices to ensure all electrical work is done safely and up to code, giving you confidence in your home or business's electrical systems.
Personalized Service: We believe in one-on-one service. Every project is unique, and we tailor our solutions to your specific needs, offering transparent communication and cost-effective options.
Timely and Reliable: We respect your time, and we'll work diligently to meet deadlines and complete your project in a timely manner.
Customer Satisfaction: We take immense pride in our work, and your satisfaction is our ultimate goal. We're not just completing a project; we're building long-lasting relationships with our clients.
